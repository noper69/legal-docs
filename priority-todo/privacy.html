PRIORITY Privacy Policy Effective Date: April 15, 2026  ·  Developer: John Persha This Privacy Policy describes how Priority ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is written plainly and completely — there are no hidden practices. 1. Information We Collect Priority does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The App has no backend, no user accounts, and no registration process. The only data the App stores is saved locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults framework. This includes: • Your tasks and their text content • Reminder dates and times you have set • Your selected color scheme preference This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. 2. Data We Do Not Collect We do not collect, process, or have access to: • Your name, email address, or any contact information • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or advertising IDs • Location data • Usage analytics or behavioral data • Crash reports sent to third parties 3. Notifications Priority uses Apple's local notification system to deliver reminders you schedule. All notifications are generated entirely on your device — no notification content is sent to or processed by any external server. You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone's Settings app under Notifications → Priority. 4. No Third-Party Tracking or Analytics Priority contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking libraries. We do not use services such as Firebase, Facebook SDK, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any equivalent. No data is shared with, sold to, or accessed by any third party for any commercial or analytical purpose. 5. Third-Party Links The App contains an optional link to a Ko-fi page for voluntary support. Tapping that link opens a third-party website in your browser and is governed entirely by Ko-fi's own privacy policy. No data from Priority is transmitted when you access that link, and tapping it is entirely optional. 6. Local Storage All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. Uninstalling the App will remove this local data. We have no ability to access, retrieve, or restore locally stored data. 7. Children's Privacy Priority is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because no personal data is collected at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from any age group. 8. Data Security Because Priority collects no personal data and operates with no server-side infrastructure, there is no central database that could be breached. Your tasks and preferences remain entirely within Apple's device security model, protected by your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically. 10.
