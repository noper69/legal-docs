PEPTIDEHQ Privacy Policy Effective Date: April 15, 2026  ·  Developer: John Persha This Privacy Policy describes how PeptideHQ ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is written plainly and completely — there are no hidden practices. 1. Information We Collect PeptideHQ does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The App has no backend, no user accounts, and no registration process. The only data the App stores is saved locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults framework. This includes: • Your in-app preferences and settings (e.g., display options, saved reference items) • Subscription status, as confirmed by Apple's StoreKit framework This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. 2. Data We Do Not Collect We do not collect, process, or have access to: • Your name, email address, or any contact information • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or advertising IDs • Location data • Health or medical information • Usage analytics or behavioral data • Crash reports sent to third parties 3. No Third-Party Tracking or Analytics PeptideHQ contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking libraries. We do not use services such as Firebase, Facebook SDK, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any equivalent. No data is shared with, sold to, or accessed by any third party for any commercial or analytical purpose. 4. Subscriptions and Payments PeptideHQ offers auto-renewable subscriptions ($9.99/month and $49.99/year) to unlock full access to the App's reference content. All subscription purchases, billing, renewals, and payment processing are managed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We never see, receive, or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. To manage or cancel your subscription, visit your Apple ID account settings or the Subscriptions section in the iOS Settings app. Apple's privacy policy governs any data Apple collects in connection with your purchase. 5. Local Storage All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. Uninstalling the App will remove this local data. We have no ability to access, retrieve, or restore locally stored data. 6. Children's Privacy PeptideHQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is intended for adult users engaged in independent research and educational activities. 7. Data Security Because PeptideHQ collects no personal data and operates with no server-side infrastructure, there is no central database that could be breached. Your preferences and subscription state remain entirely within Apple's device security model. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
