LET IT ALL GO Privacy Policy Effective Date: April 15, 2026  ·  Developer: John Persha This Privacy Policy describes how Let It All Go ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is written plainly and completely — there are no hidden practices. 1. What You Type Is Never Stored The core purpose of Let It All Go is to give you a private space to release your thoughts. Nothing you type into the App is ever saved, stored, transmitted, or seen by anyone — including us. When your words float off the screen, they are gone entirely. There is no log, no history, no record of any kind. 2. Information We Collect Let It All Go does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The App has no backend, no user accounts, and no registration process. The only data the App stores is saved locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults framework. This includes: • A counter tracking how many free releases you have used • Subscription status, as confirmed by Apple's StoreKit framework This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. 3. Data We Do Not Collect We do not collect, process, or have access to: • Any text or content you type into the App • Your name, email address, or any contact information • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or advertising IDs • Location data • Usage analytics or behavioral data • Crash reports sent to third parties 4. No Third-Party Tracking or Analytics Let It All Go contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking libraries. We do not use services such as Firebase, Facebook SDK, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any equivalent. No data is shared with, sold to, or accessed by any third party for any commercial or analytical purpose. 5. Subscriptions and Payments Let It All Go offers auto-renewable subscriptions ($4.99/month and $24.99/year) to unlock unlimited use of the App. All subscription purchases, billing, renewals, and payment processing are managed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We never see, receive, or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. To manage or cancel your subscription, visit your Apple ID account settings or the Subscriptions section in the iOS Settings app. Apple's privacy policy governs any data Apple collects in connection with your purchase. 6. Local Storage All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. Uninstalling the App will remove this local data. We have no ability to access, retrieve, or restore locally stored data. 7. Children's Privacy Let It All Go is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. 8. Data Security Because Let It All Go collects no personal data and operates with no server-side infrastructure, there is no central database that could be breached. Your preferences and subscription state remain entirely within Apple's device security model. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically. 10.
